About me

My name is Garba Joseph Samson. I am a passionate student, creative thinker, and aspiring entrepreneur. I am currently studying Software Development at Brigham Young University–Idaho, where I am learning how to use technology to solve real-life problems. My education is very important to me, and I always look for ways to apply what I learn in practical ways.

Outside my studies, I have worked on several projects as a graphic designer and video editor. I have created educational content, designed video courses, and built a growing brand called Doxa Pâtisserie. Through Doxa Pâtisserie, I focus on producing healthier baked goods that are both elegant and enjoyable. It is a brand that reflects my love for creativity, wellness, and adding value to people’s lives.

I enjoy learning, building new ideas, and working with others who are passionate about growth and impact. If you are interested in creative design, healthy baking, education, or software development, I would be happy to connect. Let us share ideas, learn from one another, and create something meaningful together.
